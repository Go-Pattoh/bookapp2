Help & Support

How do I save a book?

Simply click the "Download" button on any book card. This will save the book to your personal library. You can view your saved books in the "My Library" section.

I can't log in. What should I do?

Ensure you are using the correct email and password. If you registered recently, try refreshing the page. If the issue persists, please contact support.

Where are my downloaded books?

Books you "Download" (save) are stored in your account. Click on "My Library" in the sidebar or the user menu to see them.

How do I view a book?

Click the "View" button on a book card to open its details page or preview on Google Books.
